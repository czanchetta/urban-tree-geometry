# Limitations

- H∞ and k are **adopted**, not locally calibrated; no statistical confidence
  interval.
- Heights and crowns are **not field-measured**.
- Crowns are assumed **circular** (X = Y).
- Pruning, topping, urban conditions and site alter the relationships.
- Palms and incomplete identifications: **low confidence**.
- Very large DBH (> ~120 cm) is an **extrapolation**.
- **Not** for arboricultural report, removal, compensation, stability or hoisting.

Trees near luminaires should be **measured in the field**. See
`docs/limitations.md` in the repository.
